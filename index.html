How to play:

Roll the dice and make your way to the top. Hopefully you don’t land on a snake!

- Select your color, roll the dice and, move your piece. If you roll a 6, you get another roll for free.
- Climb ladders - dodge snakes There are ladders and snakes of varying lengths scattered around the board. Landing on a snake causes you to slide down the board, while you can climb up ladders you land at the foot of the game.

Snakes and Ladders is a chance-based board game featuring 100 squares. Players have to get to the top while dealing with the consequences of every dice roll. Historically, the game was used to teach children basic moral values. The ladders were representative of virtues while the snakes of vice. Today, you can see it as a metaphor for life’s journey with all of its ups and downs.
